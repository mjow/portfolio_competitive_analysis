Android

Website: www.android.com

Brand:

Technical and practical, but the Android Icon gives a playful and less serious feeling to the website.

Layout:

Four Boxes

Functionality:

The layout of the site is easy and clear, it is broken into three major parts: Discover Android, Get Apps, and Develop Apps. The three parts are the information that the visitor most concern: to understand the brand, how to get it, and how to develop it. The simpleness of the layout makes it easy for the vistors to choose the information they want to view and will not be confused.

Interactivity:

On the front page of the site, there are some bubbles of pictures that you can click on and will be a brief description of the information. The visitor can click on those bubbles to quickly browse through the important information on the website. There aren't that many interactivity functions on the website.

Navigation:

The navigation of the website is smooth and clear. It is easy to quickly find the information that you need and quickly get to the information.
